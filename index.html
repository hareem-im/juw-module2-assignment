Chicken

Heat oil in the wok and the garlic and ginger paste and stir fry for few seconds. then add crush coriander and cumin and stir well. now add chicken 1-2 minutes and add one chilli with ine slit down and fresh corriander and mix it well. Add roughly grind tomatoes, salt, crush red chilli and turmeric. Cook covered with lid till the chicken is done. Garnished with sliced ginger and green chilli. Serve hot with chapati and naan.

Beef

Beef yakhni pulao is wonderful pulao recipe in which rice cooked in beef stock. On Eid ul Azha Pakistani people love to make it with fresh meat and it tastes really good when cookes with fresh meat. You can also make it with lame and goat meat.
